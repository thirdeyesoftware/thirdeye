Wireless Hardware
 
iobeam Service Gateways
In order to provide wireless internet access, you will need to install one of our industry leading service gateways in your venue. iobeam offers two different service gateways depending on your wireless needs and budget.
 
iSG
The iSG is a powerful wireless access gateway that provides a wireless internet signal in your indoor venue. It is truly a plug and play device. Take it out of the box, plug it into your network, turn it on and your venue can host wireless customers immediately. No additional setup is required!

The iSG includes the following features:
» High powered industry standard 802.11b (or 802.11g) radio
» High gain adjustable antenna (2nd antenna optional)
» DHCP or static IP address capable.
» Additional ethernet port for daisy chaining access points or connecting a private network.
» Customizable Splash Page
» Network Availability Schedule
» Web Content Filtering **
 
iSG Pro
The iSG Pro includes all of the features of the iSG. In addition, it includes the following:
» Optional second wireless radio for separate/private wireless traffic.
» Content Filtering
» MAC Address Filtering
» Port Blocking
» Walled Garden
» Network Activity Monitoring (graphs and reports)
» Remote 24/7 Management and Monitoring by iobeam Network Engineering
